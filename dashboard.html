Pop Out
All-teams stream mission — every scrobble counts
How to Play
HQ drops a Pop Out — one song or album to spin, on the clock (usually 6h). Hit Join and stream it with every other team. The moment the squad smashes the goal, a 🔮 Magic Box bursts open for everyone who joined — surprise rewards inside! 🎁 Get at least 7 streams in to claim yours. Beat the clock! 🎯💜

Just Dive
Decode the BTS song from its initials — one shot per day
How to Play
A BTS song title is scrambled into just its initials — like S D for Spring Day. You get one chance to type the full song title. Get it right and earn +8 XP. Runs daily — a new song drops every midnight KST! 🎵
⚖️
Still Spinning
Pick a side — the carousel never stops
How to Play
A spicy BTS dilemma drops every day — two options, pick one. There's no right answer! After you vote, see immediately how everyone else in the app voted. The real fun starts in the GC once everyone has picked a side 👀
🔗
Eat This Life
Instinctive word relay — pass it on, one word per day
How to Play
Your team builds a word chain together — each word must start with the last letter of the previous word. You get one word per day. Longer chains = more team XP! Coordinate with your squad so no one runs out of ideas 🔗
⚡
That's Fire
5 BTS questions — 7 seconds each, answer before it burns out
How to Play
5 BTS trivia questions back to back. The moment a question appears, a 7-second countdown starts. Type your answer and press Enter — or time runs out and you skip it. Results and XP revealed at the end. One round per day, new questions every midnight! ⚡

📻

Many of you asked for a reliable backup for your streams — something community-first. We heard you.
