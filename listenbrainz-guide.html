What is ListenBrainz?

Meet ListenBrainz

ListenBrainz is a free, open-source music tracking platform run by the MetaBrainz Foundation — the same non-profit behind MusicBrainz, the open music database used across the industry. It was built to be free forever, with no ads, no paywalls, and no commercial strings attached. Your listening data is yours — always.

Setup Guide

Get ListenBrainz in 5 Minutes

Follow the steps for your device. The scrobbler apps below can send plays to multiple services at the same time — so nothing you already have gets disrupted.

1
Create your ListenBrainz account All devices
Step 1: Go to listenbrainz.org → Sign In. It will redirect you to MusicBrainz to create an account — this is normal, they are the same organisation. Sign up, verify your email, then log in.

Step 2: Once logged in, go to listenbrainz.org/settings → scroll down to Connect services → connect your Spotify account (click "Activate both features"). Your streams will now be recorded automatically.

Step 3: Scroll further down on the same settings page to find your User Token → copy it. You'll need this for the scrobbler app in the next step.
2
Install a scrobbler app iOS
Scrobbles for Last.fm (App Store) — supports multiple scrobbling services including ListenBrainz. Go to Settings → add ListenBrainz with your username + token.

OpenScrobbler — browser-based, works on any device at openscrobbler.com.

Apple Music users on iOS: Use the free Shortcuts automation on the ListenBrainz website → listenbrainz.org/add-data
2
Install a scrobbler app Android
Pano Scrobbler (Play Store, free) — the best Android option. Supports Spotify, YouTube Music, and most streaming apps. Add ListenBrainz in Settings → Services → ListenBrainz → enter your token.

Simple Scrobbler (Play Store, free) — lightweight alternative that also supports ListenBrainz.
3
Connect Spotify directly Optional — All devices
ListenBrainz has a built-in Spotify connection — no separate app needed.

Go to listenbrainz.org/settings/music-services/details → click "Activate both features (recommended)" under Spotify → log in with your Spotify account. Done — your streams will be recorded automatically from that point on.
4
Add your ListenBrainz username to HopeTracker All devices
Once you're scrobbling to ListenBrainz, go to your HopeTracker Dashboard → Profile page → scroll to the ListenBrainz Preview section → enter your ListenBrainz username → click Check →

You'll see a preview of your streams matched against this week's targets. This is a verification tool for now — we'll announce when it becomes an official sync source.

⏳ First time? It can take up to 1 hour for your streams to appear after connecting Spotify. If counts show 0, come back later and hit Check again.

FAQs

Common Questions


Do I have to switch to ListenBrainz?
No. Your current scrobble setup stays exactly as it is. ListenBrainz is optional — set it up if you want a reliable backup, or skip it entirely. Nothing changes in HopeTracker unless you choose to connect it.
Can I use it alongside my current scrobbler?
Yes — this is the recommended setup. Apps like Pano Scrobbler (Android) and Scrobbles (iOS) can send every play to multiple services at once. You add ListenBrainz once and it runs quietly in the background.
Is ListenBrainz available in India?
Yes, fully. ListenBrainz has no geo-restrictions anywhere in the world. It's run by a non-profit — not a commercial company — so there are no regional blocks or paid tiers.
Will my stream history carry over?
Your XP and stream history are already saved in HopeTracker — nothing is lost. If you want your full listening history on ListenBrainz too, you can import it at listenbrainz.org/settings → Import.
I set it up — now what?
Go to your Profile page in the dashboard → scroll to the ListenBrainz Preview card → enter your username → hit Check. If the numbers look right, you're all set. We'll announce when ListenBrainz becomes an official sync source.
